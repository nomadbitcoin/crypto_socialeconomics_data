With several CryptoNote firsts such as uncle mining via the <a href="https://github.com/masari-project/research-corner/blob/master/secor/secor.pdf" target="_blank">SECOR protocol</a>, a <a href="https://wallet.getmasari.org" target="_blank">fully client side web wallet</a>, and PoW sharding via the <a href="https://github.com/masari-project/research-corner/blob/master/blocktree/blocktree.pdf" target="">blocktree protocol</a>, Masari is a fast, fungible, secure, private and soon-to-be scalable
