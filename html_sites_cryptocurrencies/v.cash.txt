It inherits Bitcoin\xe2\x80\x99s distribution mechanism and leverages on Grin\xe2\x80\x99s privacy protocol by using Mimblewimble implementation.\n    <br/>\n<div style="padding-top: 16px"></div>\n    VCash adopts Merged Mining to allow mining of VCash and Bitcoin simultaneously with the same \xe2\x80\x98Proof-of-Work\xe2\x80\x99 (POW) algorithm. Blockchains that utilize POW algorithm are susceptible to the risk of 51% attack on the network. However, Bitcoin has the largest number of miners worldwide and widely distributed hashing power, which significantly lowers the risk of a 51% attack. With Merged Mining, VCash is able to leverage on Bitcoin\xe2\x80\x99s hashing power with the support from Bitcoin mining pools. Therefore, VCash possesses the same level of security as Bitcoin and launching a 51% attack on Vcash network would be very costly and, therefore nearly impossible.\n    <br/>\n<div style="padding-top: 16px"></div>\n    The goal of VCash is to be the most private and secure chain for storing values.\n  </div>\n<p class="title-h3 hover-to-underline" style="    margin-bottom: 125px;">\n<a href="/about">more&gt;&gt;</a>\n</p>\n</div>\n<div class="bg-gray">\n<div class="wrapper" style="position:relative; top: -160px;">\n<p class="title-h1" style="margin-bottom: 35px">Why VCash?</p>\n<div class="card">\n<div class="card__item">\n<div class="card__item-logo img-mining"></div>\n<div class="card__item-title">Merged Mining</div>\n<div class="card__item-content">\n          Through Merged Mining with Bitcoin, VCash is able to leverage on Bitcoin\xe2\x80\x99s hashing power with the support from Bitcoin mining pools.\n          Therefore, VCash possesses the same level of security as Bitcoin and launching a 51% attack on VCash network would be nearly impossible.\n        </div>\n</div>\n<div class="card__item">\n<div class="card__item-logo img-security"></div>\n<div class="card__item-title">Privacy</div>\n<div class="card__item-content">\n          VCash is integrated with MimbleWimble technology which is the one of the leading privacy protocols.
