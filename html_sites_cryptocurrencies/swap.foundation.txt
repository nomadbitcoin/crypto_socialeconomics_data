Only signing users have knowledge of\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\tsender and receiver.</p>\n<p>Ring signatures restrict double-spending attempts by linking transactions with the same\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\tprivate key.\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\tThe protocol uses the key image, derived from a private key through an unidirectional\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\tfunction.</p>\n<p>Users store all images and verify them against new transactions.\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\tIf there is a double key image, the transaction is rejected as a double-spending attempt.\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\tMeanwhile the identity of the sender remains unknown, it\'s not possible to get a private key\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\tfrom an image.</p>\n<p>The protocol generates multiple unique one-time addresses for each payment from the unique\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\tpublic key.\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\tThe sender uses random data and the receiver\'s public address to generate a one-time key.\n\t\t\t\t\t\t</p>\n<p>The redeemable funds require the receivers private key. They are the only one who can receive\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\tthe funds sent to the one-time address.\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\tA third party can\'t see the connection between the one-time key and the public address of\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\tthe receiver.</p>\n</div>\n</div>\n<div class="col-12 col-md-6">\n<div class="faq__item">\n<h4>Supply and emission</h4>\n<p>Genesis on November 16, 2018, at 09:06:03 (UTC).</p>\n<p>Total supply is 18,400,000 XWP.</p>\n</div>\n<img alt="Emission Chart" class="img-fluid img-chart" src="images/media/swap-emission.png"/>\n<div class="faq__item">\n<h4>Tail emission</h4>\n<p>When the block reward is below tail emission block reward, the tail emission phase will\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\tbegin.
