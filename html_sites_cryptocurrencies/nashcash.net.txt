class="col-lg-6 order-lg-1" id="mining">\n<div class="p-5">\n<h2 class="display-4" style="color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-size: 45px;">The mining</h2>\n<p style="font-size: 20px;font-family: Roboto, sans-serif;color: rgb(255, 255, 255);">Mining is an essential part of protecting the NashCash network. NashCash uses Proof of Work (mining). The miners solve a block by computing tasks and thus confirm its correctness. If the block is solved and correct, it is added\n                            to the blockchain and the transactions it contains are executed. The first transaction in a block is always a so-called coinbase transaction.
