Most of current solutions are either commercial centralized services \n                                or are based on volunteer networks which are prone to misuse and censorship. Too fill the gap and provide secure, trustless solution the project take an advantage of developments in distributed systems and blockchain technology,\n                                to build economically self-supporting and eventually self-governing network, significantly more resistant to disruptions or or censorship.<br/>\n                                The network currently provides decentralized VPN and tunelling as well as decentralized recursive DNS.
