class="col-xs-12 col-sm-4">\n<div class="info-group text-center">\n<h5 class="color-smalt-blue" localization="why-myriad-multi-algorithm-title">Multi-Algorithm</h5>\n<p localization="why-myriad-multi-algorithm-text">Myriad is the first currency to support 5 algorithms, catering for ASIC, GPU and CPU mining. Currently, those algorithms consist of <b>SHA256d</b>, <b>Scrypt</b>, <b>Myr-Groestl</b>, <b>Argon2d</b> and <b>Yescrypt</b>.<br/><br/>Myriad\'s multi algorithm approach offers exceptional 51% resistance because a range of different hardware is needed to find 51% of the blocks.</p>\n</div>\n</div>\n<div class="col-xs-12 col-sm-4 text-center">\n<div class="info-group">\n<h5 class="color-coral-tree" localization="why-myriad-merge-mining-title">Merge Mining</h5>\n<p localization="why-myriad-merge-mining-text">Merge mining is allowed on the <b>SHA256d</b> and <b>Scrypt</b> algorithms. This has enabled Myriad to take advantage of the huge hashrates currently in use by coins using these algorithms.<br/><br/>Merge Mining, or AuxPow, allows the work being done for a parent coin (such as Bitcoin or Litecoin) to be used to find valid blocks on Myriad\'s blockchain.</p>\n</div>\n</div>\n<div class="col-xs-12 col-sm-4 text-center">\n<div class="info-group last">\n<h5 class="color-purple-mountains-majesty" localization="why-myriad-distribution-title">Distribution</h5>\n<p localization="why-myriad-distribution-text">Myriad was released to the wild on February 23, 2014 at 18:30 UTC. The <a href="http://insight-myr.cryptap.us/block/cb41589c918fba1beccca8bc6b34b2b928b4f9888595d7664afd6ec60a576291" target="_blank">first mined block</a> came 4 minutes later, thus establishing that there was no premine.<br/><br/>The initial reward was 1,000 MYR, halving every 967,680 blocks (approximately 48 weeks). Total supply is approximately 2 billion MYR. Target block time is 60 seconds, or 300 seconds per algorithm.</p>\n</div>\n</div>\n</div>\n</div>\n</div>\n</div>\n</div>\n<div class="section section-2 row" id="wallets">\n<div class="col-xs-10
